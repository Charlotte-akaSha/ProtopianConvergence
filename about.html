Our Story

Protopian Convergence is an invitation to explore the inner and outer system changes needed in our organizations and our behaviors to upgrade our civilization towards pro-equality and regenerative societal systems.

What We Do

We bring together changemakers, activists, and regenerators to collaborate, mastermind, and prototype solutions to the world's most critical issues. Through immersive experiences, we explore new models for economy and society that are equitable and regenerative.

Our Approach

We believe in incremental progress—making things better today than yesterday, even if not perfect. This realistic approach to change allows us to make meaningful progress without falling into the trap of utopian thinking that often leads to disillusionment.

Join Us

If you're a changemaker, activist, or regenerator working towards a more equitable and regenerative future, we invite you to join our community and participate in our events.
